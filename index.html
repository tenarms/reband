About Us

Founded in 1991, R.B. Hash is an established, full service roofing contractor specializing in satisfied customers. Meeting and exceeding your every need is our commitment to you. We provide the strongest work force in the roofing industry, and all of our employees (management and crew) are highly skilled and have been trained extensively in their specialized fields.

We know that the best roof system ever designed will not do the job if it is not properly installed. That is why only R.B. Hash crews install R.B. Hash roofs. We hire our crews. We train our crews. We insure our crews. We stand behind every job with a satisfaction guaranteed policy.
